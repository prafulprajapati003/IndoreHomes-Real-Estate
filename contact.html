Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How do I list my property on IndoreHomes?

Listing your property is simple! Visit our "Sell" page, fill out the property details form, upload photos, and submit. Our team will review and activate your listing within 24 hours. You can also call us directly for assistance.

Are there any charges for listing properties?

Basic property listings are completely free! We only charge a small commission when your property is successfully sold through our platform. Premium listing options with enhanced visibility are available at nominal charges.

How do you verify property listings?

Our team conducts thorough verification of all property listings. We verify ownership documents, visit the property, and ensure all information provided is accurate. Only verified properties receive the "Verified" badge on our platform.

Can I schedule a property visit online?

Yes! You can schedule property visits through our website, mobile app, or by calling our office. Simply select the property, choose your preferred date and time, and we'll coordinate with the property owner to confirm the visit.

What areas do you cover in Indore?

We cover all major localities in Indore including Vijay Nagar, Super Corridor, Rau, Nipania, Palasia, Bhawarkuan, Bengali Square, AB Road, Scheme 140, MR 10, and more. Our network extends to both residential and commercial properties across the city.
